Application-Specific Tunnels

Bind session encryption to individual apps or domains. Engineers can protect code repositories while letting streaming services route directly, maintaining speed without compromise.

Whole-Device Mode

With one toggle, extend the mesh to the entire computer. Sentinel enforces AES-256-GCM encryption with Perfect Forward Secrecy, auto-negotiating the best protocol per device.

Layered Security Stack

Combines IKEv2, WireGuard, and proprietary tunneling with optional Onion Router handoffs. Users can chain routes for high-risk assignments or diplomatic travel.

Kill Switch and Session Pinning

Sentinel monitors gateways and instantly blocks traffic if the tunnel drops. Session pinning protects cookies and tokens by limiting them to the secure channel.

Cookie and Tracker Sanitization

Every protected domain receives automatic cookie rotation, tracker stripping, and script isolation to prevent session hijacking or data leakage.

Telemetry and Insights

Network dashboards reveal throughput, blocked anomalies, and handshake performance. Export metrics to your SIEM or send alerts to Slack, Teams, or ServiceNow.

Adaptive trust engine

Keeper evaluates every connection for posture anomalies. If a device leaves an allowed geofence, fails compliance checks, or experiences suspicious data spikes, Sentinel quarantines the session and rotates exit nodes automatically.
