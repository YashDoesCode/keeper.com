Technology

Cryptography, identity, detection, and privacy

Keeper documents its model choices transparently. The stack below reflects the target capabilities of the product suite.

Password Manager

AES‑256 and ChaCha20‑Poly1305 with PBKDF2 and Argon2id. Zero‑knowledge SRP‑based login.

  • Zero‑knowledge proofs
  • SRP verifier login
  • Anomaly detection (IForest/LSTM‑AE)
  • Scrypt
  • Shamir’s Secret Sharing

2FA & Identity

Phishing‑resistant MFA and passkeys.

  • HOTP/TOTP
  • FIDO2/WebAuthn
  • OAuth2/OIDC/SAML
  • Rotating JWKs
  • Risk models & biometrics
  • Device fingerprinting, PIM

Account Management

Continuous governance, audit, and adaptive controls.

  • RBAC/ABAC/PBAC
  • CAE
  • Audit telemetry
  • Insider detection (GNN)
  • Session anomaly (LSTM/transformer)
  • Token binding, scoped tokens

Crypto

Wallet and key management.

  • BIP‑32/39/44
  • Multisig
  • TSS/MPC
  • Secure chips, enclave MPC

VPN

Private networking with fast protocols.

  • WireGuard, IKEv2/IPsec
  • TLS 1.3 PFS
  • ChaCha20/Curve25519
  • Multi‑hop, fingerprint randomization
  • Shadowsocks/obfs4

File Encryption

Device and cloud encryption.

  • TPM sealing, XTS‑AES
  • Secure Boot, PCR attestation
  • Enclave keys, rotation
  • Client‑side AES‑GCM, DEK/KEK, KMS
  • Blind index (FHE/PEKS)

Antivirus

Static and behavioral defenses.

  • Heuristics, sandboxing, EDR
  • IDS/IPS, CNN/BERT malware
  • Phishing classifiers, syscall anomaly
  • Kernel hooks, forensics
  • Cert pinning, RAG intel

Stealth Workspace

Hidden encrypted workspace and volumes.

  • Hidden volumes
  • MPC wallet signing
  • BIP‑32/29/44, UBA
