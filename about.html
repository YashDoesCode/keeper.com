About Keeper

Our mission is to secure your digital life with innovative and user-friendly solutions.

Our Story

Keeper was founded with the vision of making cybersecurity accessible and effortless for everyone. In an increasingly digital world, the need for robust personal security has never been greater. We believe that protecting your online identity and sensitive information should not be a complex task reserved for experts, but a fundamental right for all.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a security platform that unifies essential protection tools into a single, elegant experience. From secure password management to advanced malware scanning and file encryption, Keeper is designed to offer comprehensive defense without compromising on speed or usability.

Our Values

Privacy is the cornerstone of our commitment to you. At Keeper, we implement zero-knowledge architecture along with advanced on-device cryptography to ensure that your data remains accessible only to you, providing the highest level of security and confidentiality. Our design philosophy centers on creating seamless and elegant user experiences, ensuring that complex security processes are intuitive and effortless to navigate. We believe that robust protection should enhance your workflow without slowing you down or introducing unnecessary complexity. The cybersecurity landscape is ever-changing, and we continuously invest in research and innovation to stay ahead of emerging threats, ensuring your digital safety evolves with the times. Transparency lies at the heart of our relationship with users. We are dedicated to fostering trust through clear, honest communication about our practices, security measures, and how your data is protected, so you can have confidence and peace of mind when using our services.
