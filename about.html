About Keeper

Our mission is to secure your digital life with innovative and user-friendly solutions.

Our Story

Keeper was founded with the vision of making cybersecurity accessible and effortless for everyone. In an increasingly digital world, the need for robust personal security has never been greater. We believe that protecting your online identity and sensitive information should not be a complex task reserved for experts, but a fundamental right for all.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a security platform that unifies essential protection tools into a single, elegant experience. From secure password management to advanced malware scanning and file encryption, Keeper is designed to offer comprehensive defense without compromising on speed or usability.

Our Values

  • Privacy First: Your data privacy is our top priority. We employ zero-knowledge architecture and on-device cryptography, ensuring that only you have access to your information.
  • Simplicity & Elegance: We strive to build intuitive and beautiful interfaces that make complex security tasks simple. Protection should never slow you down.
  • Innovation: The digital threat landscape is constantly evolving, and so are we. We are committed to continuous research and development to stay ahead of emerging threats.
  • Transparency: We believe in open communication with our users. Our practices are designed to be clear and understandable, fostering trust and confidence.

Meet the Creator

Keeper is a personal initiative by Yash Srivastava, an AI/ML undergraduate with a passion for cybersecurity and visual storytelling. Drawing from early coding experiences and a background in VFX/SFX, Yash aims to make cybersecurity simpler and more human. The focus is on practical security—fast, intuitive, and reliable—built for students, creators, and engineers who need speed without compromise.
