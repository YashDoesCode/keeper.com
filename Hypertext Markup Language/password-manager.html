Password Manager

Zero-knowledge password manager using Argon2id + SHA256-Bit-CBC encryption. Store and manage all your passwords securely with offline export, breach alerts, and passkey-ready authentication.

Argon2id + SHA256-Bit-CBC Encryption

Industry-leading encryption algorithms ensure your passwords are protected with military-grade security. Argon2id provides protection against GPU cracking attacks, while SHA256-Bit-CBC ensures data integrity.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Your master password never leaves your device. All encryption and decryption happens locally, ensuring that even Keeper cannot access your passwords. Your data remains private and secure.

Offline Export

Export your passwords securely for backup purposes. All exports are encrypted and can only be accessed with your master password, giving you complete control over your data.

Breach Alerts

Get instant notifications if any of your saved accounts are compromised in a data breach. We monitor dark web databases and alert you immediately if your credentials are found.

Passkey-Ready Authentication

Future-proof your security with passkey support. Store and manage passkeys for passwordless authentication across all your devices and services.

Password Generator

Built-in password generator creates strong, unique passwords for all your accounts. Customize length, character types, and complexity to meet your security requirements.

Secure Sharing

Share passwords securely with family members or team members. All shared passwords are encrypted and can be revoked at any time, maintaining your security.

Cross-Platform Sync

Access your passwords from any device. Sync seamlessly across macOS, Windows, Android, and iOS, ensuring your passwords are always available when you need them.

Auto-Fill Integration

Seamless browser and app integration automatically fills your passwords when you visit websites or use applications, making security effortless and convenient.
