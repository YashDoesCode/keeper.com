2FA & Passkey Authenticator

Two-factor authentication and passkey support for enhanced security. TOTP/Push notifications with offline backup codes and seamless device transfer.

TOTP Authentication

Time-based One-Time Password (TOTP) support for all major services. Generate secure 6-digit codes that refresh every 30 seconds, providing an additional layer of security.

Push Notifications

Receive instant push notifications for authentication requests. Approve or deny login attempts directly from your device, making two-factor authentication seamless and secure.

Offline Backup Codes

Generate and store backup codes for emergency access. These codes work even when you don't have access to your authenticator device, ensuring you're never locked out.

Passkey Support

Store and manage passkeys for passwordless authentication. Passkeys provide the highest level of security using cryptographic keys that are unique to each device.

Seamless Device Transfer

Transfer your authenticator accounts between devices securely. All 2FA tokens are encrypted and can be exported and imported safely, ensuring you never lose access.

QR Code Scanning

Quickly add new accounts by scanning QR codes. The setup process takes seconds, making it easy to secure all your online accounts with two-factor authentication.

Multiple Account Management

Manage unlimited 2FA accounts in one place. Organize your accounts by category, search quickly, and access all your authentication codes from a single, secure location.

Cross-Platform Sync

Sync your 2FA tokens across all your devices securely. Your authenticator accounts are encrypted and synced, ensuring you have access from anywhere.

Secure Backup

Automatically backup your 2FA accounts with end-to-end encryption. Restore your authenticator accounts on any device, ensuring you never lose access to your secured accounts.
