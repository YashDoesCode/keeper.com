About Keeper

Our mission is to secure your digital life with innovative and user-friendly solutions.

Keeper was founded with the vision of making cybersecurity accessible and effortless for everyone. In an increasingly digital world, the need for robust personal security has never been greater. We believe that protecting your online identity and sensitive information should not be a complex task reserved for experts, but a fundamental right for all.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a security platform that unifies essential protection tools into a single, elegant experience. From secure password management to advanced malware scanning and file encryption, Keeper is designed to offer comprehensive defense without compromising on speed or usability.

Our Values

At Keeper, we implement zero-knowledge architecture along with advanced on-device cryptography to ensure that your data remains accessible only to you, providing the highest level of security and confidentiality. Our design philosophy centers on creating seamless and elegant user experiences, ensuring that complex security processes are intuitive and effortless to navigate.

We believe that robust protection should enhance your workflow without slowing you down or introducing unnecessary complexity. The cybersecurity landscape is ever-changing, and we continuously invest in research and innovation to stay ahead of emerging threats, ensuring your digital safety evolves with the times.

Transparency lies at the heart of our relationship with users. We are dedicated to fostering trust through clear, honest communication about our practices, security measures, and how your data is protected, so you can have confidence and peace of mind when using our services.

Meet the Creator

Yash Srivastava is an eighteen-year-old self-taught coder recognized for his perfectionist attitude and strong focus on productivity. He specializes in programming languages such as Python, Java, HTML, and LaTeX and began his coding journey in 2020 while in the eighth grade.

Beyond programming, Yash is a skilled editor with experience using Adobe Creative Suite, including Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Lightroom, and Audition, as well as Canva. This combination of technical knowledge and creative skills gives him a unique ability to tell compelling visual stories and manage post-production tasks efficiently.

Keeper is a personal project developed by Yash Srivastava, an AI and machine learning undergraduate with a passion for cybersecurity and visual storytelling. His early experiences in coding and a background in visual and special effects have driven his mission to simplify cybersecurity through fast, intuitive, and reliable solutions.

Yash's journey into cybersecurity and cryptography began in 2021 when a popular password manager was hacked, resulting in the loss of all his stored accounts. This experience inspired him to deepen his knowledge and led to the creation of his own cryptographic model. This model reflects Yash's commitment to privacy, security, and empowering users to protect themselves effectively.
