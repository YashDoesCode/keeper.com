Web Guard & Ad Blocker

Chromium-based browser extension with safe site indicators, custom ad blocking, phishing protection, LAN intrusion prevention, and configurable filters.

Safe Site Indicators

Visual indicators show the security status of websites you visit. Green indicators show safe sites, while warnings appear for potentially dangerous or suspicious websites.

Custom Ad Blocking

Block ads across all websites with customizable filters. Choose which types of ads to block and customize your browsing experience to be ad-free and distraction-free.

Phishing Protection

Advanced phishing detection identifies and blocks malicious websites designed to steal your credentials. Get instant warnings when visiting known phishing sites.

LAN Intrusion Prevention

Protect against local network attacks and prevent unauthorized access to your device from other devices on the same network. Keep your data safe from local threats.

Configurable Filters

Customize filtering rules to meet your specific needs. Block trackers, malicious scripts, and unwanted content while allowing trusted websites to function normally.

Tracker Blocking

Block tracking cookies and scripts that follow you across the web. Maintain your privacy and prevent advertisers from building profiles based on your browsing behavior.

Malicious Script Protection

Automatically block malicious JavaScript and other scripts that could harm your device or steal your information. Browse safely without worrying about malicious code.

Privacy-First Design

All filtering and blocking happens locally on your device. Your browsing data never leaves your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Cross-Browser Support

Works with all Chromium-based browsers including Chrome, Edge, Brave, and Opera. Install once and enjoy protection across all your browsers.
